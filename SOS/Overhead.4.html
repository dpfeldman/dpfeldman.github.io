Liberal Arts Colleges


  1. Take courses outside your area of expertise.

  2. Research:

    • Suitcase and/or BIG physics is bad.

    • "Be able to find and fund a sustainable undergraduate research program."

  3. Many liberal arts colleges do not require a post-doc.

  4. Don't overlook visiting positions!

  5. There are roughly 100 openings per year at baccalaureate degree institutions.
