Abstract for Bennett Corrado's Talk

This talk will be a review of the information I have acquired in the past year as Chair of the Graduate Student Association, and during my internship in Washington D.C. this summer. I will be summarizing some of the recent reports from the NRC and NSF concerning graduate education in the physical sciences, and updating y'all on what the campus plans to do with this information. Also, I will tell of what I saw happening on Capital Hill this summer regarding funding for and attitudes toward basic scientific research.
