Applying for Teaching Positions:

A Report form Reno

I recently attended the American Association of Physics teachers winter meeting in Reno, NV. I attended several talks focusing on how graduate students can prepare themselves to be competitive for teaching faculty positions.

I will begin by discussing some general advice which is of relevance for applying for teaching positions at a wide range of institutions. I will then discuss some issues specific to different types of institutions: community colleges, liberal arts colleges, state colleges, and private high schools.
