Community Colleges


  1. ALL research must focus on pedagogy.

  2. All classes at first and second year level.

  3. Small classes, enormous diversity in ability and learning styles.

  4. CC are open to all students.

  5. 2/3 of community college physics departments are 1 person!

  6. There are between 100 and 150 applicants per position.

  7. Between 2000 and 2010, approximately 1/2 of all CC physics teachers will retire. On average, 70/year.

  8. An M.S. is all that's required to teach at a CC in most states.
