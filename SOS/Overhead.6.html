Private High Schools


  1. Similar to Community Colleges.

  2. I've known quite a few people who left CC's and state schools to teach HS.

  3. Might have to coach, deal w/ parents, lead field trips, run clubs, etc., etc.

  4. Opportunity to teach lots of different classes.

  5. LOTS of opportunities for innovation and pedagogical research.

  6. Be careful, the character of private high schools ranges widely.
