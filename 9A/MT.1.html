Comments on the First Midterm


A sample midterm will be available at Navin's Wednesday afternoon.

Logistics:

  • The exam is in Roessler 66, (where we usually have class).
  • You do not need to bring blue books
  • The exam is closed book; closed notes.
  • The exam is 50 minutes long. There are approximately six problems.
  • The problems have unequal point value. Pay attention to this and use your time wisely.
  • On most problems, in order to receive full credit, you must show your work. (If no work is shown you will surely receive no partial credit!!)
  • We will not provide equations on the exam, so you should be sure to know the basic kinematics equations, Newton's laws, etc.
What the exam will cover:
  • All of chapters 1, 2 and 3. Chapter 4, up to and including section 4-4.
  • The exam will NOT cover the normal force.
  • There is no need to memorize conversion factors.
  • Your study time would be best spent going over homework problems, discussion section problems, supplementary problems, and topics discussed in lecture.
Here's where you can find help if you need it.

Good luck!
