Comments on the Final


We will not make a sample final. The overall style of the final will be very similar to the midterms.

Logistics

  • The exam is Wednesday, June 12, 8:00--10:00 am in Roessler 66.
  • You do not need to bring blue books.
  • The exam is closed book; closed notes.
  • The exam is 2 hours long. There are approximately thirteen problems.
  • The problems have unequal point value. Pay attention to this and use your time wisely.

What the exam will cover

The final will be comprehensive, but will emphasize the material covered since the last midterm. Roughly 2/3 of the final will be on material that we've covered since the second midterm.

The following sections will not be on the exam:

  • ch 8 sections 4,7,8,9
  • ch 9 sections 4,5
  • ch 10 sections 1,3,5,6,7
  • ch 12 section 5
Sections 7,8 of ch 12 (damped and forced oscillators) will be on the exam only in the form of qualitative questions. You will not need to know the detailed equations.

Topics not covered on Midterm I or Midterm II will, of course, not be covered on the exam.

If you use an equation other than those which we have discussed and/or derived in class, you must provide a derivation or justification to receive full credit.
