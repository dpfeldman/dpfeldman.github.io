Some Foundations in Complex Systems: Entropy, Information, Computation, and Complexity.

David P. Feldman


I have developed a series of lectures which I delivered at the Santa Fe Institute's annual Complex Systems Summer School in Beijing China from 2004 to 2008. A list of topics covered is below. Here are the slides from the two most recent versions of the lectures:
  • Beijing 2008 [pdf] (17MB, 295 slides)
  • Paris 2007 [pdf] (17MB, 293 slides)
The Paris lectures were given at the Complex Systems Summer School, Institut des Systemes Complex de Paris Ile-de-France in Paris.
