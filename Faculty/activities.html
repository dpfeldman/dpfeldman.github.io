Partial List of Recent Faculty Activities

College of the Atlantic

This is only a partial list. Several faculty did not have the time to add their activities to this list. It is designed to be representative, not exhaustive, and is indented to give a broad overview of the range and extent of professional, research, creative, and community activities in which College of the Atlantic faculty participate. This list was compiled by David Feldman (faculty moderator, 2001-2002), and is also available online at: http://hornacek.coa.edu/dave/Faculty/activities.html.
