Books for Thorndike


High Priority

  • Helen Plotz, Imagination's Other Place: Poems of Science and Mathematics. 1955.
  • Karl L. Niklas Plant Biomechanics: An Engineering Approach to Plant Form and Function. The University of Chicago Press, 1992. ***
  • Taylor and Wheller, Spacetime Physics. Freeman. ***
  • Silvanus Phillips Thompson, Calculus Made Easy. St. Martin's Press. 1988. ISBN: 0312114109.
  • Richard Wolfson. Nuclear Choices : A Citizen's Guide to Nuclear Technology (New Liberal Arts). MIT Press. 1993. ISBN 0262731088.


Medium Priority

  • Alan, Jeffrey, Handbook of Mathematical Formulas and Integrals. Academic Pr; ISBN: 0123825806.
  • David J. Griffiths, Introduction to Electrodynamics. Prentice Hall.
  • Harry M. Schey, Div, Grad, Curl, and All That: An informal Text on Vector Calculus. Norton. 1997. ISBN: 0393969975.
  • Roger S. Jones, Physics for the Rest of Us: Ten Basic Ideas of Twentieth-Century Physics That Everyone Should Know...and How They Shaped Our Culture and Consciousness. NTC/Contemporary Publishing. ISBN 0809237164.
  • Daniel F. Styer. The Strange World of Quantum Mechanics. Cambridge University Press. 2000. ISBN: 0521667801.


Lowish Priority

  • A few large, good, calculus-based physics texts. *** (Important to get sometime, but definitely not worth buying new.)
  • Ramamurti Shankar, Basic Training in Mathematics : A Fitness Program for Science Students. Plenum Publishing. 1995. ISBN: 0306450364.


Atomic Bomb Books

  • Paul S. Boyer, By the Bomb's Early Light : American Thought and Culture at the Dawn of the Atomic Age. Univ. of North Carolina Press. 1994. ISBN: 0807844802.
  • Paul S. Boyer,out : A Historian Reflects on America's Half-Century Encounter With Nuclear Weapons. Ohio State Univ. Press. ISBN 0814207863.
  • Peter Bacon Hales, Atomic Spaces : Living on the Manhattan Project . Univ. of Illinois Press. 1999. ISBN: 0252068319.
  • Kai Bird and Lawrence Lifshultz (eds.), Hiroshima's Shadow: Writings on the Denial of History and the Smithsonian Controversy. Pamphleteers Press. 1998. ISBN 0963058738.
  • Richard Wolfson. Nuclear Choices : A Citizen's Guide to Nuclear Technology (New Liberal Arts). MIT Press. 1993. ISBN 0262731088.
Books denoted with a stars (***) were suggested by students.
