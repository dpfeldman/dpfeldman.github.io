Photos of FTAA Protests in Quebec City

April 19-22




A line of riot cops. A line of police formed to the right (from the point of view of the previous pictures which were looking at the wall from the outside.) Shortly after this picture was taken, police launched canisters into the middle of the crowd instead of just using tear gas right by the wall. The crowd fled backwards (tear gas isn't fun) and the police moved forward.
