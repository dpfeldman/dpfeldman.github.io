Monster


Monster is large. She eats a lot and thus is big. She got her name becuase she reminds us of the Cookie Monster. She likes to purr, eat, and sleep. She really likes those large twisties that come wrapped around heads of lettuce. We crumple it up, and she plays with it for hours, batting it along the floor or grabbing it in her mouth and flinging it toward the sky.
