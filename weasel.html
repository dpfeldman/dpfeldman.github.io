Weasel


We call her weasel, because, well, she kinda looks like a weasel. Weasel is quite skitterish; she spends a lot of time under the bed and behind the bathtub. She used to be afraid of the ceiling fans, although she's more or less gotten used to them by now. It's an uneasy truce. Weasel is rarely seen by visitors.
