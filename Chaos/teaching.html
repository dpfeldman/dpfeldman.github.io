Online courses:

I have developed two MOOCs that cover many of the topics that are included in my textbook.

  • Introduction to Dynamical Systems and Chaos
  • Fractals and Scaling

Both courses are offered on the Santa Fe Institute's Complexity Explorer platform. All course materials are free, and archived versions of the classes are available when the courses are not in session. Students and instructors will find lots of material, including videos and some online quizzes, that complement the material in the text.

These two classes are currently the two highest-rated math MOOC according to class-central.com, a MOOC aggregation and rating site.
