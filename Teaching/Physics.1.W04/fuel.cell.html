Hydrogen Fuel Cell Kit


Your main task is to get the hydrogen fuel cell kit to work. There's an instruction booklet that you should follow. You'll need to set up a time with me to get the fuel kit.

Your write-up need not be extensive, but it should contain a sketch of the apparatus and a brief discussion of how it works. You should also make a few measurements. How much gas did you get, and how long did it take to make? How long did the fan run using this gas?

Find at least one recent journal, magazine, or newspaper article about fuel cells. This shouldn't be hard. What are people saying about fuel cells these days?
