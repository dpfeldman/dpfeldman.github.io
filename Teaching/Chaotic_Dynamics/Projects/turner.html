Fractal Concepts in High School Math and Literature

Rita Turner


For my project, I will design a method of instruction to teach fractals to high school students, examine how to incorporate fractal concepts into the content and instruction of literature and writing (in high school or elsewhere), analyze the ways that fractal concepts can be translated to the act of writing, and write sample fractal poetry. Through a poster and presentation, I will explain and show examples of my high school curriculum and activities as well as fractal writing.
