Voters and Surface Tension: patterns of Voter Cellular Automata

Teak Wall


For my project I will have a poster and do a talk. The poster is going to have three or four color print-outs comparing some two-dimensional majority rule CAs to some pictures (maybe water droplets, etc.) that will help me to explain whta the patterns may mean (i.e., if it looks like there is surface tension in the CA as in water, then it may be saying something about the dynamics of the CA--maybe there is tension at the edges of groups in the majority rule model?). On this poster, I will describe some rudimentary aspects of what a CA is, mostly what a 2-d CA is and include some text about the pattern analysis. For the talk I would like to begin with a very brief introduction to 2-d CAs and then move into the basics of what majority rule CAs are all about and then mostly talk about different patterns and rules within this category and what they may mean.
