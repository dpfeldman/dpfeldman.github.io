Diffusion in What!?

Aaron Shepard


My project is analyisis on a study conducted to find an adequeted model which describes stream fish diffusion. In combination of actual data and partial differential equations they were able to come up with a model that similated diffusion fairly well. There model was also able to take in to account letptokurtosis, which is movement distribution with unequal variences.
