Harvesting the Green Sea Urchins of Maine

Jenn Sweeney


Abstract: The focus of this project was to look at harvesting equations and to apply it to actual fisheries in Maine, focusing on green sea urchins. The purpose was to test how practical these equations are and what factors could be changed to make the equation more realistic.
