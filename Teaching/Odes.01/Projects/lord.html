The Schrodinger Equation and Three Applications

Sam Lord


In this presentation, I explore tow applications of the Schrodinger equation: the "free particle," the "particle-in-a-box." These applications demonstrate some aspects of quantum mechanics using differential-equation techniques we have studied throughout the term. I will show how the Schrodinger equation and differential equations can be used to model and study topics in physical chemistry.
