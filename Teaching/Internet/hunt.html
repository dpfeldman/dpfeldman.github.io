Web Surfing Exercise


For class on Thursday you should do the following two explorations. We will discuss your experiences in class.

  1. In class, you will be assigned some topic to research/explore on the web. Spend at least an hour finding out about the topic. Be prepared to discuss the sites that you found and how you found them.

  2. Come to class prepared to briefly discuss a few (1 - 3) of your favorite websites. You should say why you like them, and how you found them. Bring the URL with you to class.
