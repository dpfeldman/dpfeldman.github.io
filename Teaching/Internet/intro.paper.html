Introductory Paper Guidelines


For this assignment, write a paper of at least two pages in which you address the following:

  • What big questions and concerns do you have about the internet?
  • Do you have any strong feelings -- hopes or fears -- about the internet?
This is sort of a "why do you want to take the class and what do you want to get out of it" essay. Your writing can be quite informal, but should be clearly written. Don't worry about writing a polished essay. The idea is to take a "snapshot" of your ideas, questions, and feelings about the internet.
