Odes: Useful Links


This list isn't at all comprehensive.

  • Boston University ODES project The homepage for our text.

  • Maple Applications. A set of differential equations applications for Maple. Could be some excellent resources here.

  • IDEA: Internet Differential Equations Activities, "an interdisciplinary effort to provide students and teachers around the world with computer based activities for differential equations in a wide variety of disciplines." It looks like they have some great material for interesting projects, buildup of toxins, chemical reactions, rangeland ecosystems, salmon migration, small mammal dispersion, etc. Definitely worth checking out!

  • The Connected Curriculum Project at Duke has some interesting modules on applications of differential equations.

  • Some differential equations projects and other resources are available at Gavin Larose's DiffEQ Resource Page

  • Internet Resources for Differential Equations at Swarthmore's math forum. Many excellent links here.
