What's a colophon?

Wikipedia's entry defines a colophon as "A brief description usually located at the end of a book, describing production notes relevant to the edition." I got the idea to add colophons to some webpages from Claire Congdon, whose explanation of a colophon is interesting and makes good sense to me.
