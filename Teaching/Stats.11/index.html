References and Resources

This is not intended to be a comprehensive list.

Textbooks

  • Peter Dalgaard, Introductory Statistics with R. Springer, 2008.

  • Derek Ogle, Notes for Introductory Statistical Analysis. An excellent introductory text, uses R. Available free online.

  • Michael Lavine, Introduction to Statistical Thought. Another good book available as a pdf. Emphasizes math and probability a little more than Ogle.

  • Ben Bolker, Ecological Models and Data in R. Princeton University Press. See also Bolker's website for the book.

  • W.N. Venables, D.M. Smith, and the R Development Core Team, An Introduction to R. A short introduction to R by the R development team.

  • John Verzani, simpleR---Using R for Introductory Statistics.
