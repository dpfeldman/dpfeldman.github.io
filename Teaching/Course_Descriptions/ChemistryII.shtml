Introduction to Chemistry II

Instructor: Don Cass

This course is the continuation of Introduction to Chemistry I. It moves from using atomic structure to explain chemical reactivity to using thermodynamic concepts to understand the extent of different reactions. This course uses extensive algebra. Intermediate. Prerequisite: Introduction to Chemistry I or equivalent. Offered every year. Lab fee $25. *ES* *QR*
