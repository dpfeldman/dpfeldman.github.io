Advanced Analysis in Biology

Instructor: Sean Todd

This course builds on Introduction to Statistics, introducing more advanced statistical techniques within the field of parametric statistics, in particular multiple regression and advanced forms of analysis of variance used in biologically oriented studies. Labs will focus on computer assisted statistical analysis, and reporting style. Evaluation will be by two quizzes and several choices of take-home problems. Intermediate/Advanced. Prerequisite: Introduction to Statistics, or permission of instructor.
