Final Exercise


Due at 5:30pm on the last day of the term.

The purpose of this exercise is for you to reflect on your initial ideas about gender, science, and their interrelationship. We could like you to re-read your first essay and critique, respond to, and expand upon, your initial comments. In so doing, you should build upon the readings and discussions we've had this term; be sure to refer to at least three of the readings we've done.

We'd like you to re-read your essay and re-read and reflect upon some of the readings we've done. However, you should spend only three hours in writing your response. You may have a writing tutor look over your writing if you wish. We encourage you to discuss your ideas with others while you're preparing the essay. You final essay must be typed and attached to your original essay. Please put the essays in Dave's mailbox.

If you have any questions or concerns about this assignment, please contact us.
