Guidelines for leading discussion


You should choose a topic and be prepared to lead a class discussion on it.

  • Please clear your topic with Suzanne or Dave.
  • To prepare, you should read at a bare minimum three articles or book chapters on the topic. (You should discuss your reading list with us.)
  • Choose a reading that the class will do. If this reading isn't in our reader, put it on reserve enough in advance so that others can read it. This reading should be about twenty pages.
  • Use the common reading as a basis for discussion. However, you should be prepared to bring in additional information and ideas from the other reading you did.
  • You should plan on your discussion lasting around 40 minutes.
  • You should also prepare and hand in to us an annotated bibliography.
