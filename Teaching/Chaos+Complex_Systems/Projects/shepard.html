The Two- and Three-Body Problems


My project is based on the relationship between the two body and three body problem, namely why one is solvable and the other isn't. To properly illustrate this I will be going over the history of the two and three body problem as well as showing what happens between various intial conditions in each situation. I will also include pictures showing what happens with each intial condition given.
