Ben Volta

Fractal Networks as a Model for River Basin Development


This project examines the use of network and fractal models to explain the creation and growth of river basins. It is an attempt to understand if and how some order can be found in the apparent randomness of the dynamics behind drainage networks. By looking at the variety of factors that concur into shaping a river basin and representing them in a mathematical model, we can try to comprehend the complex interactions between chance and physical causality that are at the heart of the river's development.
