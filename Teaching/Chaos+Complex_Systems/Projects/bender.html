Evan Bender

Chaos in Music


How does the human creative process work? Do we ever unconsciously produce chaos- or fractal-related art? Can chaotic, fractal, or stochastic mechanisms ever replicate or imitate human creativity? Or even if they can't, can they facilitate the production of aesthetically pleasing music? These questions will be examined, and a selection of fractal music will be presented.
