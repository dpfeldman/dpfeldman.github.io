Social Networks at COA


In my project, the social network analysis I have chosen is information flow in College of the Atlantic. With this, I will look at a number of different properties networks have. For instance, I will look at properties like "In-Degree" "Out-Degree" which are associated with directed networks. I will also look at how clustered COA community is. At the bottom line, there will be all kinds of interpretations like "who is the most well known?"
