Physics I Labs


Lab Policies

  • Failing to attend labs will negatively affect your course grade and evaluation. Labs are an important (and I hope fun) part of the course.

  • In the labs you will learn how to use the computer mathematics program Maple. You will need to use Maple for some of your homework problems.
  • Typical labs will be around 1.5 hours.

  • Labs will not meet every week.
