Bookmarks for Job Searching


General Academic Job Search Advice

  • The Chronicle of Higher Education: Academe This Week Academic openings in many fields. Mainly community colleges and four-year teaching universities. Lots of other good academic job info.

  • One Page Guides An extremely helpful and entertaining collection of one-page guides to giving a talk, writing a PRL, interviewing, etc. Written by John Wilkins, a physics professor at Ohio State. Lots of this information is physics specific, but most of the advice would apply to most fields.

  • How to Get a Teaching Job at a Primarily Undergraduate Institution. Some advice put together by Malcolm Campbell.

  • Tips for a Massive Academic Job Search. Some good information and advice compiled by Ellen Spertus.


Physics Jobs

  • Physics Job List. An archive of physics job postings from a Univ. of Illinois mailing list.

  • The OSU Physics Career Counseling Page. More useful stuff from Ohio.

  • Werner Koepf: How I found my job. A tale of how a theoretical nuclear physicist made the transition to industry.

  • Academic and Research Positions in Physics. Maintained by Prof. Y.S. Kim at the University of Maryland, this site has many job listings in the US and all over the world.

  • Notes on Applying for Postdoctoral Positions. Good advice from Tim Evans and Jonathan Halliwell, Theoretical Physicists at Imperial College, London.

  • Jobs in physics, astronomy, and other fields. A huge list containing many, many links. Highly recommended. A good place to start.

  • AIP Job listings. Many academic and industry job listings.
